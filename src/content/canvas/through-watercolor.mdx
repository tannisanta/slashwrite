## Watercolor

<p>I enjoy watercolor because it reminds me that we are capable of many things and that our experiences shape us, allowing us to learn in unexpected ways. I am early in my watercolor journey, and it teaches me patience, flexibility, and how to see possibilities in unexpected ways.</p>

<p>Watercolor and technical writing have more in common than one might imagine. Both rest on a foundation of process, clarity, flexibility, and attention to detail. They require breaking down complexity, building meaning step by step, and refining continuously until the essential message shines through.</p>
